## Errores de compilación en tests (8 tests, todos E0599)

Todos los errores son del mismo tipo: tests que llaman a funciones `async fn` (`process_story()`, `run_real()`) sin `.await` y sin estar en un contexto `#[tokio::test]`.

### STORY-011 (3 tests)

| # | Test | Línea | Función llamada | Problema |
|---|------|-------|-----------------|----------|
| 1 | `process_story_accepts_shared_state` | L1887 | `process_story(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |
| 2 | `process_story_blocked_returns_early` | L1909 | `process_story(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |
| 3 | `process_story_failed_returns_early` | L1930 | `process_story(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |

### STORY-012 (5 tests)

| # | Test | Línea | Función llamada | Problema |
|---|------|-------|-----------------|----------|
| 4 | `run_real_with_terminal_stories_completes_in_one_iteration` | L2593 | `run_real(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |
| 5 | `run_real_with_no_stories_completes_immediately` | L2633 | `run_real(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |
| 6 | `run_real_with_draft_story_invokes_po_path` | L2680 | `run_real(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` en `is_ok()` |
| 7 | `run_real_with_draft_story_invokes_po_path` | L2683 | `run_real(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` en `unwrap()` |
| 8 | `run_real_shared_state_reflects_sequential_processing` | L2712 | `run_real(...)` | `async fn`, falta `.await` |

### Solución requerida

Cada test necesita:
1. Cambiar `#[test]` a `#[tokio::test]`
2. Añadir `.await` en cada llamada a función async

Ejemplo:
```rust
// Antes:
#[test]
fn test() {
    let result = process_story(...);
    assert!(result.is_ok());
}

// Después:
#[tokio::test]
async fn test() {
    let result = process_story(...).await;
    assert!(result.is_ok());
}
```
