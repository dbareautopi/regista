## Errores de compilación en tests (11 errores)

### Grupo A: Tests síncronos que llaman funciones async sin `.await` (8 errores E0599)

#### story011 (3 tests)

| # | Test | Línea | Problema |
|---|------|-------|----------|
| 1 | `process_story_accepts_shared_state` | 1880 | `process_story(...)` → `.is_ok()` sobre `Future`, falta `.await` |
| 2 | `process_story_blocked_returns_early` | 1902 | `process_story(...)` → `.is_ok()` sobre `Future`, falta `.await` |
| 3 | `process_story_failed_returns_early` | 1923 | `process_story(...)` → `.is_ok()` sobre `Future`, falta `.await` |

#### story012 (5 tests)

| # | Test | Línea | Problema |
|---|------|-------|----------|
| 4 | `run_real_with_terminal_stories_completes_in_one_iteration` | 2586 | `run_real(...)` → `.unwrap()` sobre `Future` |
| 5 | `run_real_with_no_stories_completes_immediately` | 2626 | `run_real(...)` → `.unwrap()` sobre `Future` |

**Causa**: `process_story()` y `run_real()` son ahora `async fn`. Estos tests mantienen
`#[test]` (no `#[tokio::test]`) y no usan `.await`.

### Grupo B: Test asíncrono con future no-`Send` (3 errores en 1 test)

| # | Test | Línea | Error |
|---|------|-------|-------|
| 9 | `process_story_does_not_block_runtime` | 2286 | `dyn Workflow` no implementa `Sync` |
| 10 | `process_story_does_not_block_runtime` | 2286 | `Box<dyn AgentProvider>` no implementa `Send` |
| 11 | `process_story_does_not_block_runtime` | 2286 | `dyn AgentProvider` no implementa `Sync` |

**Causa**: `tokio::spawn` requiere `Future: Send` pero los traits `Workflow` y
`AgentProvider` no tienen bounds `Send + Sync`.
